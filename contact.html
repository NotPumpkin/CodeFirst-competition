About Us

This website was created by two students of the University of Glasgow participating on a Code First Girls: Web Programming Beginners Course. On this page you can get to know a little bit more of the developers and contact us.


Carolina Linhares

Carolina is a MSc. Sustainable Energy student at the University of Glasgow. She is from Brazil and her background education is in Civil/Mechatronics Engineering. Very passionate about technology and innovation, Carol believes new technologies can help us change the world. "Renewable energy is a very important part of the smart cities of the future." Join us being a supporter of sustainable energy!


Gabrielle Appleby

Gabrielle is a final year BSc Psychology and Neuroscience student at the University of Glasgow, currently working on her dissertation project about the perception of pain, and that's probably why she looks like she hasn't slept in three days (she hasn't). Gabrielle hopes that sustainability initiatives will help protect the world for the enjoyment of future generations.
